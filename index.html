Help/Instructions

Begin by clicking the until you have enough money to get your first job.
After that you can choose to wait to be able to get another job or do some stuff on the side to make more money.

Each job/promotion gives you money per second as well as influence per second.
Influence is needed to get a better job.

You can access this menu again by going to the options tab!
